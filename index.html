Menu

Our soups are available from our fresh brand new soup bar. Your choice of our assortment of fillers and broths, that are delicious and tasty. Allowing for you to make a soup to your every wish.

Salads and Pastas

Our salad and bar has a large assortment of ingredients with many different dressings and sauces, both creamy and vinaigrette based. The delicious bar has both cold and hot ingredients for your desired flavour combos. There are also gluten-free options available for your pasta needs.

Wraps and Sandwiches

Our wraps and sandwiches are available with a large assortment of ingredients and you have your choice of size and many different flavours of bread and wraps. Gluten-free options of spinach wraps are available for those who are gluten-intolerant.

Health

Whether you're going meat-free, gluten-free, organic, for health, environmental or animal-welfare reasons, being a healthy doesn't mean you're stuck with salads. Our menu is filled with tasty choices you (and your taste buds) can really get excited about. At Marketplace, to qualify for our healthy category, each item must be free of meat, gluten or pesticide sources, including wheat, meat, fish and shellfish. Milk and egg products, and enzymes or rennet from animal sources are allowed.

Our Story

We have a thing for stories.

We tell ours through our fresh ingredients and our passion for the plate. Twenty-five years ago, the Marketplace story began in Ottawa on Woodroofe Avenue. Today, we serve the thousands of students that come thirsting for knowledge to our College.

Our exceptionally talented Executive Chef brings unexpected yet tasty twists to classics. He then gives these carefully crafted recipes to his culinary team, making sure they have perfected these creations before bringing them to you. And that's what you can always be sure of, that there's a chef in every Marketplace kitchen making your plate just right.

It's this passion in our recipes that makes a visit to Marketplace truly unforgettable. Whether you come for a favourite meal or love trying new and trendy dishes, Marketplace is all about great food without the pretension. We're an impromptu meeting place for friends and a cozy spot for date nights. We're familiar and friendly, but always on the cutting-edge. We're nights to remember and remarkable flavours to savour. That's how we set the table at Marketplace.
